OUR BUSINESS

Our products are made with both quality and sustainability in mind. Our partners and Crocycle work together to transform the recyclable materials into an assortment of tools, for example plastic into crochet hooks, stitching markers, needles. We would like to provide quality tools while also aiding efforts to reuse wastes that still have use, instead of leaving them to remain debris. In Crocycle, we are a crochet business that uses recyclable materials to upgrade your style.
